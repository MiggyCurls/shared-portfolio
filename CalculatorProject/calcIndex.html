The calculator can perform basic functions, such as: multiplication, addition, division, and subtraction. The calculator can perform these such operations with parenthesis to indicate the desired order of operations; however, the parenthesis cannot go deeper than one level. An example can be: (2 * 3) + (3.14 / -2). The calculator will perform the calculation when the '=' is pressed gathering what is in the screen and outputing to the screen. The '±' will be used to the change the sign of the leftmost digit on screen, if you so wish to change any of the signs of the other numbers, opt for clicking the '-' instead.
